# fixed: holiday is every year on the same month and day of month.
# arguments: month, day, name of holiday
#
# float: holiday is floating.
# arguments: nth, day of week, month, day of month, name of holiday.
#
# Day of week is a case-insensitive string, first letters are sufficient,
# e.g. MONDAY, TUESDAY, WEDNESDAY, THURSDAY, FRIDAY, SATURDAY, SUNDAY.
#
# Meaning holiday is on the nth day of week after given month and day of
# month. If nth is negative then holiday is before. if day of month is
# negative, then last day of month is used.
#
# easter: holiday is after or before easter sunday.
# arguments: positive or negative number of days, name of holiday.
#


# improved syntax for floating holidays: 
# float 4 SUN before 12 24
# float 4 SUN before 12 last-day
